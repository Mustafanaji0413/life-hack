Kitchen Hacks

Place a chalkboard and hangers on the inside of your kitchen shelf to store measuring cups the fashion way.

Store shelves in your shelves to optimize spacce and to better take advantage of the empty space not used.

Fill an ice tray with coffee so that you can enjoy your coffee without watering it down.

Put magnets under your spices to make them stick to the fridge.

Using your sink as a temporary chopping surface frees the rest of your counter for other cooking steps.

Fill a tall glass of water and lower an egg in it. If it sinks to the bottom, the egg is fresh. If it floats to the top, it's time to toss it.

bathroom Hacks

hang a shirt in the bathroom while you shower to iron it out without an iron.

Apply shaving gel to mirror and wipe it of with paper towel to stop it from fogging.

Placing your phone in the sink makes a great amplifier.

Hang small baskets on an extra shower curtain rod to free up some extra space.

Attach multiple towel racks on the back of the door to inrease space.

Apply magnetic strips behin cabninet doors to hold magnetic objects.

Bedroom Hacks

Use tension rod to make a shoe storage space.

Use a bedside caddy to store favorite items within reach.

Declutter your bedside tabletop by using pull out shelves to hold your gadgets.
