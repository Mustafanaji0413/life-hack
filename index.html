Kitchen Hacks

Place a chalkboard and hangers on the inside of your kitchen shelf to store measuring cups the fashion way.

Store shelves in your shelves to optimize spacce and to better take advantage of the empty space not used.

Stop diluting your coffee with standard ice cubes. Instead, fill an ice tray with coffee so that you can enjoy your coffee without watering it down.

Put magnets under your spices to make them stick to the fridge. That way you can multitask

Using your sink as a temporary chopping surface frees the rest of your counter for other cooking steps.
